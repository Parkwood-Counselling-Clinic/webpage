Welcome to Parkwood Counselling Clinic Webpage

The clinic provides treatment and counselling for:

1. Depression

2. Anxiety and Panic disorders

3. Social Anxiety disorder

4. Anger Management

5. Bereavement

6. Marital / Relationship Problems

7. Family Conflicts

8. Parenting Problems

9. Post-traumatic stress disorders

10. Self-esteem issues

General Information

The counsellors at the Clinic are all Medical Doctors offering treatment for a wide range of emotional and psychological disorders

The services are covered by OHIP

Referrals are invited from Family Physicians
