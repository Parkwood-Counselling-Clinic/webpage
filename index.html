Welcome to Parkwood Counselling Clinic



Our clinic provides psychotherapy for:


1. Depression

2. Generalized Anxiety Disorder

3. Social Anxiety Disorder

4. Panic Disorder

5. Obessive Compulsive Disorder

6. Anger Management

7. Bereavement

8. Marital / Relationship Problems

9. Post-traumatic stress disorder

10. Self-esteem issues



General Information


The counsellors at the Clinic are Medical Doctors offering treatment for a wide range of emotional and psychological disorders.

The services are covered by OHIP.

Please request your Family Physician or attending doctor to fax a referral to our clinic for initial consultation appointment.
